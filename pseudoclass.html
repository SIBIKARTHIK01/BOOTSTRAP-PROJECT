Descendant selectors

The Descendant selecttor matches all elements that are Descendant of a specified element.

Adjecent sibling selectors

This selector is uesed to select the element that is directly after another specific element.

Child selectors

This selector selects all elements that are the children of a specified element.

General sibling selectors

The general sibling selectors selects all elements that are next siblings of all elements.
